Events

The hostel celebrates almost all the festivals which are celebrated across the country like New Year, Diwali, Holi, Diwali, Lakshmi, Saraswathi and Ganesha etc. It is to mark the hostel day launched in the annual youth hostel magazine. A culmination of a year of school and extracurricular activities, winners were presented with gifts and prizes in recognition of their achievements on the hostel day. Every year, the Hostel Day shows not only the organizational skills, but also the active participation of all hoteliers in leisure, cultural, literary and social activities of the hoteliers coming from different parts of the world.

Fresher's Day

The hostel is a platform for students to develop all their talents. Fresher's Day is organized with a variety of entertainment programs. In fact, for many, it's a stepping stone to a good start. Various programs follow, under the expert guidance of the cultural team, directors and secretaries.

Cultural Team

The hostel's cultural team organizes fantastic cultural programs that both the participants as well as the audience inspire

Sports Forum

Sport plays a very important role in keeping everyone fit and energetic and it is a very important thing and part of young people's lives. BMSET Hostel is proud of our sports team, which on several occasions has brought together the hostel's student community through various sporting events. relevant sports kits are supplied to hoteliers to participate in various sports activities. The hostels have a gym trainer & yoga trainer who systematically guides and trains the hoteliers.

Health Forum

The hostel's Health Forum takes the initiative to create and raise health awareness among all students. Health facilities are offered at discounted rates to all hoteliers and staff as part of the BMS Hospital Trust's regular medical check-up. The services of specialist doctors can be obtained in this hospital.
