Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 Admission requirements for hostel?

A student must make sure that he has read all of the terms and conditions and produce photocopies (Xerox copies) of all the criteria for verification.

Q2. What is the procedure for seat allocation?

When there are more applications than available hostel beds, a waiting list is often created, and allocations are made in accordance with the list and any openings that may occur. Otherwise, allocations are made on a first-come, first-served basis.

Q3. How many people may share a room in a hostel?

For boys, we provide two- and four-seat rooms.

Q4. What number of meals are offered?

Breakfast, lunch, evening snacks, and dinner are among the four meals.

Q5. How is the food's quality?

Every year, the university contracts with mess facilities providers based on the criteria and performance assessment of the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India. At the time of writing, "Cater man Cuisine Concepts Pvt. The services are being rendered by Ltd. The menu is created with input from student reps and other skilled staff members. Food is always of the highest quality, and stringent sanitary guidelines are followed

Q6. What kind of fundamental infrastructure has been made available to the hosts?

Every boarder is guaranteed a basic bed with mattress, study table, chair, and cabinet in addition to air conditioning, a non-A connected bath, and any other amenities they may have selected.

Q7. Are the hostellers subject to any time restrictions?

Yes, hostellers are required to complete their outside activities for the day by 1900 hours. (7:00 PM) in the winter and 8:00 PM in the summer. In the event of any changes, the parents or guardians must notify the school at least two days in advance via the registered mobile number. This policy is carefully adhered to by the institution.

Q8. Does the hostel have a washing room?

Yes, there is a washing room in the hostel. There are few fees associated with the facility. Wet cleaning and ironing are both available.

Q9. Do you have separate mess areas for guys and girls?

Yes, there is a separate mess area for males and girls.
